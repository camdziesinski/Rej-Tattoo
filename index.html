Rej - Tattoo Artist

I'm a tattoo artist based in Tayabo, San Jose City, Nueva Ecija, currently building my experience through apprenticeship work and custom tattoo projects. I enjoy creating designs across a variety of styles, including fine line, black and grey, blackwork, ornamental, illustrative, and anime-inspired pieces.
My focus is on providing a comfortable experience while working closely with each client to create designs that reflect their ideas and personal style. Every tattoo is approached with attention to placement, composition, and continued growth in technique.
I'm currently accepting new clients and welcome both custom concepts and reference-based designs. If you're interested in booking an appointment or discussing your next tattoo, feel free to get in touch. .
